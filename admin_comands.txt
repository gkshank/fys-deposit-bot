****How to use:****
===============================

*ADMIN COMMANDS:*
1) *admin* - Show this help.
2) *edit <key> <newText>* - Edit any of these keys:
   - welcomeMessage
   - depositChosen
   - paymentInitiated
   - countdownUpdate
   - paymentSuccess
   - paymentFooter
   - fromAdmin
   - channelID
   Example:
     edit depositChosen Great, you decided to deposit {amount} Ksh!
3) *msg [254700363422,254701339573] message...* - Broadcast to multiple users.(use it at you Own risk)
   Example:
     msg [254712345678,254701234567] Hello from Admin GK-FY!
